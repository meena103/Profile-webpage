DR. ARANI ALI KHAN

Arani Ali Khan is a Assistant Professor at Indian Institute of Technology, Jodhpur. He completed his PhD from Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur in 2018. He was also formerly worked in MTRDC, DRDO as research associate and RF engineer in Amphenol omni-connect.

COURSES

Courses taught by arani ali khan

INTRODUCTION INTO ELECTRIC ENGINEERING

The goal of this course is for you (an absolute beginner) to learn all the fundamentals of Electrical Engineering in the most simple, effective and fast way.

MILIMETER WAVE TECHNOLOGY

Millimeter wave components are usually realized by frequency scaling the microwave components. However, an engineer has to deal with several design challenges in terms of higher loss, high signal-to-noise ratio, signal distortions etc.

CURCUIT THEORY

Circuit Theory is the most fundamental course in electrical engineering. what is covered In this course is a complete introduction to what electric circuits are, from the simplest one of the to some of the most complex circuits.

RF INTEGRATED CIRCUIT DESGIN

The course covers the topics on how to derive the RF wireless systems specifications, and how to design the main building blocks of a transceiver, i.e., low noise amplifier, power amplifier.
